Managing your finances

Budgeting tips for College Students and Young Adults

  1. Check your credit score regularly
  2. Take advantage of student bank accounts, usually available from age 16-24. 
    • No Monthly Account Maintenance or overdraft fees
    • Built in budgeting features
    • Credit Check Features
  3. Start saving for retirement, take advantage of any 401k and retirement planning options available
  4. Have More than one bank account 
  5. Explore Credit Card Options

  8. Look for tools to get you cash back on items you regularly purchase
  9. Keep track of your spending with both digital and cash payments
  10. Rotate streaming services, only keep subscriptions when there are shows on that service you want to watch
  11. When it comes to fees, it never hurts to ask: Banks, credit cards and other companies are allowed to waive a set number of fees
