Trying to grocery shop for yourself and or your household can be more complex than it appeared to us as kids. Adding current inflation and supply chain issues, feeding yourself and making your money stretch is more important than ever.

Couponing

One of the best ways to save money on groceries is through couponing.                                                                                      
Manager’s Specials - Manager specials are the super marked down items in stores, at most stores they will be marked with a large yellow label. Usually these items get discounted because they’re near expiration. However, markdowns also occur for cosmetic reasons, such as bruised produce, dented cans, or beat up packaging                                                                          
Ibotta- An app for earning cash back on grocery, retail, restaurant, and subscription purchases both in store and online 
Krazy Coupon Lady - A couponing and deal app                           
Flipp- An app where you can circulars for every store in your area at once, and find deals by item                                              
Grocery Store Apps- Chain grocery stores such as Walmart, Safeway, Giant, Kroger, and Winn Dixie have their own apps where you can view grocery circulars and add coupons to your loyalty account.

Food insecurity can be a difficult thing to cope with, whether it is a recent development or a more long term struggle. These organizations can help.                                                                      
FoodFinder- An organization that runs both a website and app that allows people to locate food pantries near them                      
FallingFruit.org- A nonprofit that runs a location based map of pick your own fruit trees and edible invasive plants                      
Federal Programs- TANF(Temporary Assistance for Needy Families), SNAP(Supplemental Nutrition Assistance Program), and WIC(Women, Infants, and Children)
