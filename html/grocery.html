Food and Grocery Shopping

Making A List

Making a list before grocery shopping is an important time and money saving step. Going to the store without a list can easily lead you to go over budget all while having extras you don't need and nothing you do.

The app for the store you normally shop at is a great resource for finding coupons. Many apps also have functions that allow you to build lists while showing you the price of all your items.
