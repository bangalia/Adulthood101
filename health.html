Understanding Healthcare

With laws related to healthcare and insurance plan options constantly changing, it can be difficult to make sense of all of your options

Employer Sponsered healthcare

  1. The Affordable Care Act has made it mandatory for employers with more than 50 full time workers to offer a health insurance plan. Employer sponsored health insurance can be a great deal for both you and your employer.
    • Pros:
    • Treatment for chronic conditions and mental health coverage
    • Item 3
    • Item 4
    • Cons:
    • Less options
    • Price influenced by the amount of coworkers using the same plan
    • Maintaining insurance can become costly if you use your job

Interested? Ask your employer what options they provide today.

Private healthcare 

  1. Private insurance is another healthcare choice, this is where you choose and pay for your own insurance plan directly from the source
    • Pros:
      • Customizable coverage
      • Can lead to reduced wait times for appointments and care
      • Wider range of doctors and facilities
      Cons:
    • Out of pocket costs
    • No guarantee of coverage
